Features

Everything a busy family needs

Built for real households — not classrooms. Simple enough for kids, powerful enough for parents.

🎯

Points & Rewards

Award points for positive behaviour and deduct for negative ones. Children redeem points for rewards you define — screen time, treats, days out.

🔥

Streaks & Milestones

Daily streaks keep children motivated. Milestone celebrations reward consistent effort. Watch good habits build naturally over time.

👨‍👩‍👧

Multi-child Profiles

Manage every child individually with their own goals, colour themes, and reward preferences. Sibling comparison keeps things friendly and fair.

✋

Approval Mode

Let children self-report behaviours from the kid view. Parents review and approve or reject each submission — teaching honesty alongside responsibility.

📊

Reports & Trends

Weekly summaries, behaviour trends, and reward history. Spot patterns early and celebrate progress with data-backed insights.

☁️

Cloud Sync

Your data syncs across all your devices via your own private Supabase database. No subscription required for basic sync — and your data is always yours.
